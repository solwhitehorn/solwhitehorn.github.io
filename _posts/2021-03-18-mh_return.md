### Slaying monsters again on Linux still feels really good.

---

With Monster Hunter Rise releasing at the end of the month, I wanted to dive back into Monster Hunter World. In [my last article](https://solwhitehorn.github.io/monster-hunter-proton/) I described how I had rather good performance using the custom Glorious Eggroll version of proton.

Since then, I got the Iceborne DLC, and I had a lot of fun and good time with. I played mostly online and went through most of the content proposed. Also during this time, the game got several updates adding new monsters and new events. My aim was to slay at least once every monster present in the game before I went into other things. And boy... I'm far from it. On my slaying list I still have: Furious Rajang, AT Velkhana and none other than the last true boss, Fatalis. 

Anyway, I went back into the game to slay some wyverns. A first good surprise is that the game now runs natively without having to use a custom build of proton. The other good surprise is that I'm now hitting around 70 FPS in game at max settings. Unfortunately recording the game makes it drop quite a lot to around 45 to 55 FPS. 

So as a kind of parting gift, because I don't think I'll put the time to finish the few monsters that resisted me before the release of Rise, here is some footage of me coming back to the game after a long break and having fun with Alatreon.
