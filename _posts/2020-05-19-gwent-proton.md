### Gwent - The Witcher Card Game just released on Steam. Here is how to play it in Linux

<br>

---

You must have understand from some of the videos that I posted that I really love the Witcher games made by CDPR. They released the standalone version of the Gwent game featured in the Witcher 3 back in 2017 and since then a lot has changed. 

The game was originally on GOG then ported to consoles and smartphones. Lately they are unifying the different versions of the game as well as removing the console versions. 

**Anyway, the game just released on Steam and to my surprise, it runs very well with Proton!**

If you are running the game for the first time with the current version of Proton, you will certainly have a black screen during the introduction cinematic. In my case, I'm using the [**Glorious Eggroll**](https://github.com/GloriousEggroll/proton-ge-custom) custom version of Proton (5.8) and it runs without problems.
