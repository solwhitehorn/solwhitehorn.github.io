### The custom version of proton makes miracles when it comes to playing games on Linux. Let's have some Monster Hunter<br>

---

I love Monster Hunter. I played it first on the PSP and it was a blast, but what really got me into it was Monster Hunter 4U on the 3DS. I think it spent more than 300h in this one and I have loved every single one of them.

Anyway, this time I wanted to play some with the newest Monster Hunter: **World**, a game released in 2018 for the PC. Great efforts went into having this game run on Linux and especially from the people maintaining the [**Glorious Eggroll**](https://github.com/GloriousEggroll/proton-ge-custom)custom version of Proton. 

Sure, if compared to Windows, the performance are a little bit lower and I saw my framerate drop significantly. However, I am very happy that the game runs very well. You'll see in the video that I posted that my framerate averages 45 to 50 FPS but that's while recording with OBS. Without recording, I'm a bit closer to 50 to 55 fps. I am not near a perfect stable 60FPS but I'm running the game at max settings on a Mid-spec PC that is a few years old now...

For sure I'm going to get the **Iceborne** DLC soon 🦖

Enough talking, enjoy the video and if you like it, be sure to support Proton and show Valve that you prefer to game on Linux.
