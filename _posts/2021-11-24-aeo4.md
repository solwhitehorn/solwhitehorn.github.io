### Age of Empire IV running on Linux

---

Ok. Remember 1999? Remember when Age of Empire II released and you spent your weekends playing when you just started a new year of school (at that time I was entering high-school).

Well, you are about to go back in time and dive deep into a great RTS like you love it. And to top it all, Age of Empire IV runs perfectly on Linux.

My little config is able to run it at max settings (AA disbaled) and I get above 60 fps. The game is beautiful. The gameplay is here and they made some improvement since AoE III (back to 4 resources) as well as some changes that I don't really like (very hard to micromanage and pick a single unit in a pack).
