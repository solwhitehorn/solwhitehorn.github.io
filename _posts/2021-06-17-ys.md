### Ys IX : Monstrum Nox has a demo available

---

The Ys serie is going to have a new game to be release on Steam on July 6th worldwide. Meanwhile a demo is available on the Steam page of the game.

I wanted to see if it was running on Linux and it does quite perfectly. I only had on crash at the end of my session after finishing the second part of the demo.

As always, here is a short video of how the demo runs on my system. Enjoy the video and I hope that I will be able to write a little more during this summer.
