### The Logitech MX Master 3, a linux supported wireless mouse that can do a lot!

---

Every christmas I gift something to myself and this year I decided to upgrade in the mouse departement. So far I was using a Steelseries Rival 100 that gave me a lot of satisfaction but the cable was short and I really wanted to try a wireless mouse for once. So I got myself the [Logitech MX Master 3](https://www.logitech.com/en-us/products/mice/mx-master-3.html). This is an amazing mouse, a bit pricy at $100 but you got: wireless, 7 buttons and two wheels, button gesture, USB-C and up-to 70 days of use on a full charge.
