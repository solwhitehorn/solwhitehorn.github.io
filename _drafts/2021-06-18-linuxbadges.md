### An idea for content creators 

### <br>

---

A couple of months ago I published my video showing how the Resident Evil games were running on Linux to celebrate the series 25th anniversary. 

Upon posting my video on [/r/linux_gaming](www.reddit.com/r/linux_gaming) a user had the idea to have an insert in every content creation made on linux to spread the message. After looking on the web I was not very happy with what I found. More precisely I wanted a simple image that I could insert into my videos to remind the viewer that the gameplay they were seeing was on Linux. 

Anyway, I opened Inkscape and got to work, quickly achieving this kind of 'badges' that I intend to overlay on the corner of my next videos. 

Feel free to use/modify them to your liking. Also, to all that are playing games on linux, I think that it is very important to communicate on how games runs. We all have different systems and distros and there is no better way than showing the games rather than writing about our experiences.
