テイルズオブ, the Tales of series is a franchise of J-RPG developed by Namco (now Bandai Namco) that was first released in 1995 with it's first title _Tales of Phantasia_ for the Super Famicom.

At the time, J-RPGs already had two main series driving the genre: Final Fantasy and Dragon Quest. These two franchises set the main idea of what a J-RPG would be: the story of a hero and his friends saving the world in a story-driven fantasy world where battle against foes are turn-based. 

Now when it comes to Tales, they proposed a new combat system called Linear Motion Battle System (LMBS) where the combat is more action focused with the player being able to move during battle while attacking using directional inputs. So a totally new system more akin to fighting games that gives a new dimension to what people were used to.

After sixteen main entries in the saga now comes the latest installment called Tales of Arise. The setup is simple: for 300 years a single planet, Rena, has ruled over Dahna, pillaging the planet of its resources and stripping people of their dignity and freedom. Born from those different worlds, Alphen and Shionne, each look to change their fate and create a new future. And you got to create this new future today as the game released just before I posted this article.
