### Using Steam Rom Manager to nicely organize your emulated games in Steam 

---

Not so long ago I discovered a nice little software called Steam Rom Manager (SRM). What it do is parsing through a rom or iso folder that you specified, scrap the rom's name and then add it into your Steam library as a non-Steam game.

That's it? Oh no, not only it fetches the icons, cover and art from SteamDB, il also allows you to specify the emulator and custom options you want to use to launch the rom. In other world, your Steam library can now have all of your emulated games, with beautiful artwork, and you just have to click play to launch the emulatro and the game.
