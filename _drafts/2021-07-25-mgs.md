It's been 34 years since the first release of Metal Gear on the MSX and now, more than ever, there is no better time to try and live the full experience of the saga. 

There is so much to say about Metal Gear that I don't even know where to begin. You can't deny that the series will last forever in the pantheon of stealth games. But more than the gameplay, the story and the themes conveyed through the different episodes are able to resonate will all of us on different levels.

I have always been a fan of the games. Metal Gear Solid was my first game on the PSX, and that dates back to 1999

**Metal Gear (1987) **

- emulating the MSX2, comodor 64 or NES version
- installing the GOG version with Lutris using this installation script [here](https://lutris.net/games/install/27149/view)

**Metal Gear 2: Solid Snake aka Snake's Revenge in the west (1990)**

- emulating the NES version
